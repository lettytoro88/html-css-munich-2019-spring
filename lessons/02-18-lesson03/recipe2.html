Empanadas

Ingredients

Dough

  • Flour 1 Kg
  • Butter 500 grs.
  • Eggs 2
  • Oil as needed
Broccoli

Filling

  • Red Meat 1 Kg
  • Onions 250 grs
  • Spring Onions 1 Bunch
  • Potatos 400 grs
  • Sweet Peppers 2

Procedure

Mix the ingredients until have a thick doug. Put it on the fridge for about 1 hour.
After that time, take it out and work it out until no bumps or air boils are noticeble.
Then, extend it on the counter and with a round shape cut the disks where the filling
will be put later. For the filling, cut all the ingredients in little squares and with oil
cook it at the same time on a pan until the meat is not read anymore but still juicy.
Fill each disk with a little bit of the filling, close and send to the oven for about 20 Minutes.
